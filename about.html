About Us

Read-Us Africa is a community of young Africans with a lot of potential whose vision is to nuture the African Dream with a unique identity by fostering reading skills among African Communities.

Our Goal

Foster reading skills in African Communities as a springboard for empowering potential change agents in African Soil!

-->

Why Reading Is Important

We value reading because it develops the mind of the young readers and sharperns the comprehension and their analyticial thinking abilities.

Reading is a valuable skill for acquiring knowledge and allows for a greater perspective on th world and on others. Vocabulary, grammar, and syntax are all advanced by reading, which enhances one's ability to speak and write.

Personalized Learning

Personalized learning is the skill we encourage that helps the readers be able to learn and empower students to develop thier skills in the content area.

Our Philosphy

Reading is a skill necessary for all aspect of life, without the ability to read, a person is unable to live successful life in the society

Academic Principle

Our academic Principle centers on the ability of the students to read with comprehension to be able to solve many world problems.

Key of Success
