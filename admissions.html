About Rangers Book Club

Rangers Book Club is a Youth instigated book reading program of Read-Us Africa established in over 15 African Countries and Institutions.

Vision

To establish a sustainable, high quality and accessible reading programs for all age brackets.

Mission

To foster reading skills in African Communities as a springboard for empowering potential agents in the African soil.

RBC Campuses

We foster the culture of reading amongst the different African Countries, Universities and High school that helps young people to develop great minds for the betterment of their future.

In this Campuses they are able to have regular book discussions on a weekly basis the different interesting books of recommedatin from the Read-Us Africa team.
